Meet the founder

Renata Cole brought the hotel standard home.

Before Marigold, Renata spent nine years running housekeeping for a boutique hotel in the Triangle — the kind of place where a turned-down bed and a spotless bathroom aren't nice-to-haves, they're the whole promise.

She loved the craft of it: the checklists, the consistency, the quiet pride of a room that's genuinely ready for someone. What she couldn't stand was watching that same standard disappear the moment people hired a cleaner for their own home — a different stranger each time, no system, no accountability, and no one who actually remembered the house.

So in 2017 she started Marigold to fix exactly that: a hotel-grade standard, delivered to your home by a team that learns it the way a great housekeeper learns a property — by coming back.

Renata still personally walks every new client's first clean.

Why W-2, not gig

The people in your home are our employees — not strangers off an app.

It costs us more to do it this way. We do it anyway, because it's the only way to promise the same team and the same standard, visit after visit.

The Marigold way — W-2 team

  • Hired, trained, and paid by us — held to one Marigold standard
  • The same small team returns to your home every visit
  • Covered by our insurance and bond — never your liability
  • Paid a real living wage, so good people stay for years

The gig-app way

  • Independent contractors, accountable to no shared standard
  • A different stranger most visits — you re-explain every time
  • Coverage gaps that can land on the homeowner
  • High churn — the person who learned your home is gone next month

How we vet

Every Marigold cleaner clears the same bar before they ever hold a key.

Trust isn't a tagline here — it's a process, and it's the same one for everyone we hire.

Background-checked

A nationwide criminal background check and identity verification — every hire, no exceptions, before a first shift.

Trained to one standard

New cleaners shadow an experienced team and work the Marigold checklist until every room meets it — the way a hotel trains housekeeping.

References & interview

In-person interview and reference checks. We hire for care and reliability first — the cleaning is teachable, the temperament isn't.

Insured & bonded

Full liability insurance and a surety bond cover every visit, so a rare accident is our responsibility — never your problem.

Ongoing reviews

Spot checks, your feedback after visits, and an open re-clean line keep the standard from drifting over time.

Treated like teammates

Real wages, paid training, and steady hours. People who feel valued take care of your home like it matters — because to them, it does.

The same-team promise

You'll get to know your team — and they'll get to know your home.

When you book recurring cleaning, you're matched with a small, named team that returns every visit. No "who's coming today?" text. No re-explaining where the vacuum lives or that the back gate sticks. They learn your home, your preferences, and yes — your dog's name.

If your regular cleaner is ever out, we send a teammate who's already briefed on your home and your notes. Consistency is the point, so we protect it.
